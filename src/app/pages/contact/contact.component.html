Contact FAQs

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent reports of illegal dumping, please use our hotline number for immediate assistance.

 Can I report illegal dumping anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous when reporting illegal dumping activities. However, providing your contact information helps us follow up if we need more details.

 What information should I include in a dumping report?

Please include the location, description of the waste, date/time observed, and any photos or videos if available. Vehicle information is helpful if you witnessed the dumping.
Do you offer educational programs?

Yes, we provide educational programs for schools, community groups, and businesses. Contact our education team to schedule a presentation or workshop.

 How can my organization partner with you?

We welcome partnerships with businesses, nonprofits, and community organizations. Please contact our partnerships team with details about your proposed collaboration.

 Where can I volunteer for cleanup events?

Visit our Events page to see upcoming cleanup opportunities. You can also contact our volunteer coordinator to organize an event in your neighborhood.

Need Immediate Assistance?

Our hotline is available 24/7 for urgent reports of illegal dumping activities.
