Our Story

What started as a small community initiative has grown into a nationwide movement against illegal dumping.

2015

Founded in San Francisco

Started as a neighborhood cleanup group responding to increasing illegal dumping in the Bay Area.

2017

First Mobile App

Launched our first reporting app, making it easier for citizens to document and report dumping.

2020

National Expansion

Expanded to 12 major cities across the U.S. with support from environmental grants.

2023

UN Recognition

Recognized by UNEP as an innovative solution for urban environmental challenges.
